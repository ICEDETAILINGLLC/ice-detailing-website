Platinum Detail

Best for heavily soiled vehicles or full interior restoration

Premium
Sedan $499
SUV/Truck $549
Our deepest interior and exterior reset

Our deepest package for heavily soiled vehicles, interior restoration, and longer-lasting protection.

Exterior

  • Paintwork, glass, emblems, and plastics are properly cleaned, decontaminated, and hand dried.
  • Tires and wheels are power washed, and tires are dressed to a satin finish.
  • Tires, wheels, and wheel arches are deep cleaned.
  • Black textured plastics are treated with a restoring protectant.
  • Hybrid ceramic liquid wax is machine applied for up to 6 months of protection.

Interior

  • All carpets, mats, and any cloth upholstery are vacuumed.
  • All carpets, mats, and cloth upholstery are scrubbed and steam cleaned.
  • Remaining stains are treated with a hot water extraction process.
  • Dash, console, and all vinyl are dusted and wiped down with a professional-grade cleaner.
  • Dash, console, and all vinyl are protected with a restoring UV protectant.
  • Leather is gently scrubbed, steamed, and conditioned.
  • All crevices are blown out with compressed air, and vents are deep cleaned.
  • Interior glass is cleaned.
  • High-touch areas such as the steering wheel, dash, armrests, center console, and door handles are steam cleaned, disinfected, and sanitized with 220° steam.
  • Trunk is vacuumed.
