Our Process

A consistent, paint-safe method built for luxury results.

01

Pre-Service Walkthrough

Every appointment begins with a recommended client walkthrough. We inspect the vehicle together, note concerns, and align on expectations.

02

Preparation

Safe wash and decontamination remove dirt and embedded contaminants without introducing swirls.

03

Correction

When needed, machine polishing restores gloss, reduces imperfections, and enhances paint clarity.

04

Protection

Wax, sealant, or ceramic protection is applied to lock in shine and defend against the elements.

05

Post-Service Walkthrough

We finish with a recommended walkthrough to review results together and ensure complete satisfaction.
