Our Process

A precise detailing workflow designed to deliver consistent, luxury-level results.

01

Vehicle Walkthrough

Every appointment begins with a brief walkthrough. We review the vehicle together, identify concerns, and align expectations before any work begins.

02

Safe Preparation

The vehicle is carefully prepared using proper wash methods and decontamination techniques to remove dirt and embedded contaminants without introducing swirls.

03

Interior Refinement

Interior materials are vacuumed, steamed, and treated according to surface type to restore cleanliness, comfort, and a refined cabin environment.

04

Paint Enhancement

When needed, polishing reduces swirls and light imperfections while restoring gloss, depth, and clarity to the paint finish.

05

Surface Protection

A protective layer such as wax, sealant, or ceramic coating is applied to lock in shine and protect the vehicle from environmental exposure.

06

Final Detailing

Glass, trim, wheels, door jambs, and finishing touches are refined so the vehicle leaves complete — not just clean.

07

Final Walkthrough

We finish with a final walkthrough to review the results together and ensure the vehicle meets the ICE Detailing standard.
