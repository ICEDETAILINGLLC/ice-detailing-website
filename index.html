Our Process

A consistent, paint-safe method built for luxury results.

01

Pre-Service Walkthrough

Every appointment begins with a recommended client walkthrough. We inspect the vehicle together, note any concerns, and align on expectations before work begins.

02

Preparation

Safe wash and decontamination remove dirt and embedded contaminants without introducing new swirls or damage.

03

Correction

When needed, we perform machine polishing to restore gloss, reduce imperfections, and enhance paint clarity.

04

Protection

Wax, sealant, or ceramic protection is applied to lock in shine and provide long-lasting defense against the elements.

05

Post-Service Walkthrough

We finish with a recommended client walkthrough to review results together and ensure complete satisfaction before delivery.
