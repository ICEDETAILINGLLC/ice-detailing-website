Our Story

The standard behind the name.

ICE Detailing LLC was built from firsthand experience with what clients are too often given — services that look good on the surface but fall short where it really counts.

After owning multiple vehicles and investing thousands into professional detailing over the years, one thing became clear: the quality rarely matched the price. The shine was there, but the refinement was missing. Small areas were ignored, finishing touches were rushed, and the level of care serious owners expect simply was not there.

That gap is what ICE Detailing was built to change. The goal was never to offer average work dressed up as premium service. The goal was to deliver the kind of detail, discipline, and consistency that should have been there from the beginning.

Every service is built around precision. Proper techniques, patience, and attention to the smallest areas are what separate a quick cleanup from a true detail. The areas many overlook are the areas that matter here.

Every vehicle is treated with the level of care expected on a personal vehicle, because real quality is about more than first impressions. It is about delivering results that hold up under a closer look.

Being local makes that standard even more important. Every vehicle completed by ICE Detailing represents the name, the work, and the reputation behind it.

This is not just about making a vehicle look clean.
It is about delivering the level of quality that should come standard.
