Our Story

The standard behind the name.

ICE Detailing LLC was built from experience on the customer side of the industry.

As a car enthusiast who has owned multiple vehicles and spent thousands of dollars on professional detailing over the years, I kept noticing the same thing. The results rarely matched the price. Cars would look good at first glance, but the deeper quality just wasn’t there. Missed spots, rushed work, and details that only the owner would notice were constantly overlooked.

At 22 years old, I started ICE Detailing with a simple mindset: if I wouldn’t be satisfied with the result on my own car, it’s not leaving our hands.

ICE Detailing focuses on the things most detailers skip. Proper techniques, attention to the smallest areas, and results that actually last. Every vehicle is treated like it is our own, because I know exactly what it feels like to pay for a service and expect real quality in return.

Being a local business means reputation matters. Every car that leaves ICE Detailing represents the standard I want my name associated with.

This isn’t just about making cars look clean.
It’s about delivering the level of detail car owners expect, but rarely receive.
