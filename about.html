Our Story

Built from real experience, high standards, and pride in the work.

ICE Detailing LLC was built from experience on the customer side of the industry.

As a car enthusiast who has owned multiple vehicles and spent thousands of dollars on professional detailing over the years, I kept noticing the same thing. The results rarely matched the price. Cars would look good at first glance, but look closer and the work itself didn’t reflect real attention to detail. Missed spots, rushed work, and details that only the owner would notice were constantly overlooked.

I built ICE Detailing on a standard, not a shortcut. Every vehicle must meet the same level of care, precision, and pride I would expect for my own.

ICE Detailing focuses on the things most detailers skip. Proper techniques with the highest quality products, attention to the smallest areas, and results that give you a clear mind when you step into your vehicle. Every vehicle is treated like it is our own, because I know exactly what it feels like to pay for a service and expect real quality in return.

Being a local business means reputation matters. Every car that leaves ICE Detailing represents the foundation I want our name associated with.

This isn’t just about making cars look clean.
It’s about delivering the level of detail car owners expect, but rarely receive.
