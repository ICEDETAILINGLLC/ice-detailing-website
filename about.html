About ICE Detailing

Built from real experience, high standards, and pride in the work.

ICE Detailing was built from experience on the customer side of the industry.

As a car enthusiast who has owned multiple vehicles and spent thousands of dollars on professional detailing over the years, one thing became clear. The results rarely matched the price.

Cars would look impressive at first glance, but a closer inspection revealed something different. Missed areas, rushed work, and the finer details that owners notice most were consistently overlooked.

ICE Detailing was built on a standard, not a shortcut. Every vehicle must meet the same level of care, precision, and pride that would be expected for a personal vehicle.

The focus is on the areas most services skip. Proper techniques with high-quality products, attention to the smallest details, and results that allow you to step into your vehicle with complete confidence.

Reputation matters. Every vehicle that leaves ICE Detailing represents the foundation the name stands on.
