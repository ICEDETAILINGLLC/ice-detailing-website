OUR FOUNDATION

ICE Detailing was built from experience on the customer side of the industry.

As a car enthusiast who has owned multiple vehicles and spent thousands of dollars on professional detailing over the years, one thing became clear: the results rarely matched the price.

Cars would look impressive at first glance, but look closer and the work itself often failed to reflect real attention to detail. Missed spots, rushed work, and the details only an owner would notice were constantly overlooked.

ICE Detailing was built on a standard, not a shortcut. Every vehicle must meet the same level of care, precision, and pride that would be expected for a personal vehicle.

The focus is on the things most detailers skip — proper techniques, premium products, close attention to the smallest areas, and results that give you peace of mind the moment you step back into your vehicle.

Every vehicle is treated with the same level of respect and intention that would be expected on our own, because true quality is not just about appearance — it is about confidence in the work behind it.

Being a local business means reputation matters. Every vehicle that leaves ICE Detailing represents the foundation the name stands on.
